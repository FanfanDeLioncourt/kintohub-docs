What is a KintoBlock?

What is a KintoBlock?

KintoBlocks are the new standard format for microservices. They are self-contained, containerized, universally compatible, language agnostic, combineable and shareable bricks of back-end logic.

We take off your shoulders the hassle of library configuration, deployment, and infrastructure so you can focus on writing exactly the features you need.
